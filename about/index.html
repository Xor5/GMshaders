About this website - GM Shaders is the place to learn how to create shaders for GameMaker. You can find several tutorials covering a variety of effects. There are more coming too.
Who I am - I started using GameMaker in 2010 and I've been creating shaders since 2013. I started xorshaders.weebly.com for shader tutorials while I was learning shader basics. It's time to take it to the next level with new tutorial series.
What I'm doing - This site is a work-in-progress and I will continue posting new tutorials on this website for the time being. Follow me on social media for updates.
