About this website GM Shaders is the place to learn how to create shaders for GameMaker. You can find several tutorials covering a variety of effects (more coming as well!).
Who I am I'm a GameMaker user since about 2010 and I've been creating shaders since 2013. I started xorshaders.weebly.com a year later while learning shaders and I'm now ready to take it to next level.
What I'm doing This site is a work-in-progress and I will continue posting new tutorials on this website for the time being. You can follow me on social media for updates (links on the contact page).
